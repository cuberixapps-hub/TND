Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Truth or Dare: Party Game ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Player Names: When you add players to the game, this information is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit player names to any server.

Custom Challenges: Any custom truths or dares you create are stored locally on your device. We do not access, collect, or transmit this information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Device Information: We may collect device identifiers (such as advertising ID) for the purpose of serving personalized advertisements through Google AdMob.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics through Google AdMob to understand how users interact with advertisements, including:

  • Ad impressions and clicks
  • App session duration
  • Device type and operating system version

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Game Functionality: All game data (players, scores, custom challenges) is stored locally on your device
  • To Display Advertisements: We use Google AdMob to show ads and may use your advertising ID for personalized advertising
  • To Process Payments: In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store, and we do not collect or store payment information
  • To Improve the App: Anonymous usage data helps us understand app performance and user preferences

4. Data Storage

Local Storage: All game data (player names, custom challenges, game history, scores) is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

No Cloud Storage: We do not use cloud storage or maintain servers that store your personal game data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data including:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

5.2 Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to any payment or billing information. Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

6. Children's Privacy

Our app includes a "Kids Mode" designed for children aged 7-12. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Kids Mode contains only age-appropriate content
  • No data entered in the app is transmitted to any server
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Advertisements shown in the app are configured to comply with children's privacy regulations

Parental Control: We recommend that parents supervise their children's use of the app, especially when adding custom challenges.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

  • All local data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • No personal data is transmitted over the internet
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for any network communications

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

8.1 Access and Deletion

  • Delete Local Data: You can delete all game data by uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clear Game Data: You can manually delete players and custom challenges within the app

8.2 Advertising Preferences

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Turn off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Turn off "Personalized Ads"
  • Remove Ads Completely: Purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase to remove all advertisements

9. Data Retention

Local Data: Game data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

10. International Data Transfers

As we do not collect or transmit personal data to our servers, there are no international data transfers of your game data. However, Google AdMob may transfer data internationally as described in their privacy policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using your information includes:

  • Consent: We obtain your consent for personalized advertising through the App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Legitimate Interest: We have a legitimate interest in showing ads to support the free version of our app
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide the app services you requested

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know whether we sell or share personal information (we do not)
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable as we don't sell data)
